Welcome to That Geeky Guy's Homepage!


Hi, everyone. My name is Dave and I'm the person behind this website. I am a web developer by trade and created this site to show off some of my technical skills.

While I do consider myself a developer, I am also equally invested in the design side of things. Coming from an artistic background, namely music and drawing, it is very important to me for the websites I design to be beautiful as well as functional and easy to navigate.


What can I do for you?

At this point, you are probably wondering what kind of sites I design and possibly what I can do for you. Well, if you're interested, I've designed everything from ecommerce websites to blogs and personal vanity sites. I do consulting and advising as well. One time, I had a client who ran a dating site geared toward professional women and sought my input in redesigning it.
