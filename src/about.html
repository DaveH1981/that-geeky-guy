About Me



I was born and raised in Phoenix, Arizona. I started coding in HTML and CSS in 2011 when I was looking to learn a new skill. Although I was having fun playing drums in rock bands, between that and odd jobs, I was making very little money and wanted to change that.


Although just learning basic HTML and CSS was fairly daunting at first, I soon got the hang of it and moved on to plain vanilla JavaScript. That proved even more challenging, but I persevered and it paid off. Before I knew it, I was coding entire web pages from scratch and it felt great. Not only had I discovered a new outlet for my creative energy, but I also felt a sense of accomplishment and competency with my newly acquired skillset. Most of all, making websites is fun and I'm so glad I learned how to do it.
